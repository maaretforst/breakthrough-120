SOI 5-Step Referral Engine

List them → Sort them → Tell them → Help them → Ask them. Then do it again.

1. Build the list — everyone you know, 100+ working to 200. Don't pre-judge.
2. Sort into A (closest + past clients), B (warm), C (casual).
3. Announce you're in real estate — a heads-up, not a pitch.
4. Add value before you ask — market updates, pop-bys, check-ins.
5. Ask, the right way, and make it a daily habit.

The two asks

Warm contact who already likes you — presuppose the name:

"Who do you know who's been thinking about a move?"

Cautious or cold — give them the safe out:

"Would it be a bad idea to keep me in mind if someone you know mentions buying or selling?"

Don't stack the two — pick one read of the room.

The announce call

"I wanted you to hear it from me — I got my real estate license and I'm with KW Thrive in Santa Cruz. I'm not calling to sell you anything. I just want the people I care about to know what I'm doing now."

Then shut up and let it be a normal conversation. You're planting a flag, not closing a deal.

⚠️ TCPA reminder — read before call blocks

Comply with the TCPA and all federal, state and local laws. Never call or text a number on any Do Not Call list. Don't use an autodialer, artificial or prerecorded voice without proper consent. When in doubt, ask your broker.
